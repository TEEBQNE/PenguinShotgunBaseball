The game was developed for the Ubisoft GameLab Competition in Montreal over a 10 week period. The competition required that we fit the theme of a spectacle and that the game allowed a streaming audience watching the game be able to interact with the game, affecting gameplay.

Our approach to this mandate was a custom website built from the ground up to create a chat system, currency system to purchase in game goods to affect gameplay and to purchase other cosmetic in game items. With the exception of the Twitch embedded stream, the entire web component was developed solely by me.
