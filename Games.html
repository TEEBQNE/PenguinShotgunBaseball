Penguin Shotgun Baseball (Unity C#)








Penguin Shotgun Baseball was my first game created with other game division members.
The restriction we had when designing the game is that we were only permitted to use a single input
for all controls in the game. It was a fun challenge to detect various inputs from the one key based on timing.
I only worked on this game for about a week and a half. Depending on my schedule I might return to it to fix
the couple bugs that arose and push it to a mobile market. Click the image below to play it!

Don't be Afraid of the Dark (Unity C#)










Don't be Afraid of the Dark was my next game. The restrictions for this game were a bit different than the first.
For this game, we had to base it around a particular color. Our color was white. As well, we had to target this game at
a non conventional audience. Our game is targeted at younger kids who are afraid of the dark and want to over
come this fear. My group did substantial research to find what should go in a game like this. I had a lot of fun coming up with
the various mechanics that went into it and am glad with how it turned out. I especially like the way the glow sticks
look and feel. Click the image below to play it!
